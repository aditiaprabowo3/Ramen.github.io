ABout Ramen

The noodles are characterized by their oily broth. A thick soup with a tonkotsu (pork bone) flavor and a soy sauce flavor that many noodle sellers in Tokyo sell. Ramen with a slightly thick soy sauce sauce. Soy sauce flavored sauce with onion slices.

On top of the ramen, flavorings are generally added in the form of a variety of side dishes such as: chasiu, menma, boiled eggs, green vegetables (such as spinach), sliced ​​leeks, nori, or narutomaki as a garnish.
